Welcome to Space-Wars!

Space-Wars is a game where you earn points by destroying as many asteroids as possible and combating enemy spaceships at the same time.

Levels

There are 3 levels in this game. Each level requires you to score a certain amount of points by destroying asteroids and spaceships.

Spaceships

There are a variety of spaceships available for you to suit your playstyle.

Destroy enemy spaceships!

There are enemies who will attempt to destroy you while you earn points. Destroy enemy spaceships to earn points to advance to the next level.
